The Beatles

The Beatles is my favorite band for many reasons.

First off, they have a variety of songs with many different vibes. Throughout their time making music, their music style has changed in very interesting ways.

I have never come across a song of theirs that I didn't enjoy.

Second, there is a song for every mood that you might be feeling. Since they have such a variety, I can basically always listen to them no matter what my mood is.

Lastly, I think that the Beatles have some of the most genius song writers in the band. They have great lyrics and excellent skills in creating songs.
