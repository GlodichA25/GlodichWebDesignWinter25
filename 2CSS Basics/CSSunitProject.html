Collecting Records

As someone who loves music, especially older music, collecting vinyl records is one of my favorite things to do. It is such a different and refreshing way to listen to music. The hunt for your favorite record in many stores and finally finding it is the best feeling!

Currently I have around 200 records and I have had my record player for a little over 2 years. I have a few specific places that I have gotten most of my records, which are also my top 3 favorite places to shop.

Stores

  • 1. The Town Peddler
  • 2. Dearborn Music
  • 3. Melodies & Memories
